Welcome to DOG Care

Discover Central Bark Asheville – where your dog’s best life begins! You love your furry friend, and we understand the challenge of meeting all their needs. At Central Bark, we’re here to help your dog be healthy, happy, and well-rounded. Enjoy peace of mind as your dog spends a fantastic day with friends in our safe, loving environment. Our Whole Dog Care approach focuses on nurturing your dog’s overall health and well-being throughout their life. Experience the heart of our approach with Enrichment Dog Day Care. We also provide dog boarding, grooming, training, and more, all aimed at helping you and your dog share the best life. We recognize that dogs aren’t just LIKE family – they ARE family. As fellow dog parents, we take pride in treating every dog as if they’re our own. Welcome to Central Bark Asheville!
